About us

Welcome to Twelve, where we specialize in crafting artisanal treats for the Twelve Days of Christmas. Our treats are made with 100% Australian ingredients, supporting local communities nationwide. Each delicacy is lovingly prepared by nannies who operate small businesses, ensuring every bite is infused with homemade expertise and festive cheer. From decadent chocolates to handcrafted cookies, our goodies are the perfect way to celebrate the season. Join us in spreading joy and making memories with Twelve.
